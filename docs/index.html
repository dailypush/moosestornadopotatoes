Our Story

Started in 2020, Tornado Potatoes brings a unique twist to the classic potato. Our signature spiral-cut potatoes are freshly prepared, perfectly seasoned, and fried to crispy perfection.

What began as a single food trailer has grown into Portland's favorite spot for this innovative Korean street food. We take pride in using locally sourced potatoes and creating our own blend of seasonings to give you the best possible experience.

Our Chardon Food Truck Story

From Street Food to Chardon's Favorite

Established in 2020, Mooses Tornado Potatoes brings authentic Korean street food to Chardon with our signature spiral-cut potatoes. Unlike traditional fries, our tornado potatoes are freshly prepared, perfectly seasoned, and fried to crispy perfection using locally sourced ingredients.

What began as a single food trailer in Chardon, Ohio has grown into the area's go-to destination for this innovative spiral potato treat. Our commitment to quality and unique flavors has earned us a dedicated following throughout northeast Ohio.
